About LTS

Nestled in a bustling neighborhood, Rinconcito de los Tacos charms with its vibrant murals and savory scents. Behind the counter, skilled cooks craft authentic tacos, burritos, and quesadillas bursting with flavor. Locals gather for lively conversations and shared meals, creating a sense of community. With each bite, patrons savor not just delicious food but also the warmth of tradition and camaraderie.
